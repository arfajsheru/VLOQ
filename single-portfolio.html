Development and Implementation of the E-Commerce Platform for Pestobazaar

A scalable online storefront tailored for agro and pest-control products.

Executive Summary

Pestobazaar, operated by Arbuda Agrochemicals Ltd., is a leading national distributor of premium pest-control solutions. To strengthen digital reach and streamline customer engagement, we architected and deployed a high-performance e-commerce ecosystem featuring secure payments, real-time inventory, multi-location fulfillment, and a modern storefront optimized for scale.

Background

Pestobazaar offers a wide range of genuine insecticides, gels, traps, repellents and pest-control tools for homes, commercial spaces and industrial sites. With strong demand and expanding product categories, the brand needed a robust digital presence to improve accessibility, logistics and operational efficiency.

challenges

Before the platform launch, Pestobazaar faced:

  • No Online Sales System: Limited digital reach and no integrated ordering or payment process
  • Single-Depot Fulfillment: Slower deliveries and high logistics costs
  • Scalability Issues: Manual processes for inventory and order handling
  • Inconsistent User Experience: No streamlined product discovery or tracking
  • Operational Delays: High dependency on manual order management

Impact

  • 50% Growth in Customer Base with nationwide accessibility
  • 30–40% Faster Deliveries after adding a second fulfillment location
  • 45% Increase in Online Revenue driven by seamless UX and promotions
  • Higher Operational Efficiency through automation and real-time data
  • Improved Customer Satisfaction with reliable tracking and smooth ordering

Conclusion

The Pestobazaar e-commerce platform transformed a traditional pest-control business into a scalable digital marketplace. With secure online ordering, multi-depot fulfillment, and data-driven operations, Pestobazaar is now positioned for sustained national and international growth. Future enhancements include AI-based product recommendations, more fulfillment centers, and global shipping capabilities.

Solution

We built a scalable, mobile-responsive e-commerce platform enabling seamless product discovery, ordering, and delivery. Key features include:

User-Facing Features

  • Organized product catalog with detailed descriptions and offers
  • Smooth cart and checkout flow with secure online payments
  • Customer accounts with order history and wishlist management

Fulfillment & Logistics

  • Multi-Location Fulfillment: Recently upgraded from 1 to 2 depots for faster delivery
  • Dynamic order routing based on inventory and proximity
  • Nationwide shipping, free delivery above ₹599, and live order tracking
  • Real-time inventory sync across depots

Admin & Backend Tools

  • Centralized order processing and return management
  • Automated invoices, notifications, and status updates
  • Sales and inventory analytics for better forecasting
  • Industry-standard data security and SSL encryption
